SEDS

  Growing up, I always had a passion for outer space. I loved imagining traveling to distant planets and exploring the stars. During my second year at UCSD, I discovered SEDS (Students for the Exploration and Development of Space). I joined the club in order to combine what I was learning from my computer science major along with one of my childhood passions. I was recruited to work on the CubeSat project for SEDS as a sensors specialist. More information on the project can be found here!
